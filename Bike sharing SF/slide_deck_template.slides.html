by Joana¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I'm interested in finding patterns in bike rides on this sharing system. I'll be looking at rides' duration, distance, start time. I'll also consider user types (subscribers/non subscribers).

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset contains information about 3036496 rides between April 2019 and March 2020. For each ride there's information about:

     - its duration  
     - start time  
     - end time.

For the start and end stations the dataset has:

     - its id 
     - name 
     - latitude 
     - longitude.

Finally, there is also information regarding the bike id, the type of user, whether the user used other means of transports during their trip other than bike or not, and the rental access method.

Ride duration and distance¶

Ride duration has a long-tailed distribution, with a lot of rides with shorter durations, and few with longer durations. When plotted on a log-scale, the price distribution looks unimodal, with one peak between around 500 seconds (~1.5h)
